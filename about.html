About this Website

This Is A website to Give You Some Information About the Black Lives Matter Movement!

#Blacks Lives Matter was first founded in 2013 in responce to the death of an innocent man, Trayvon Martin. A couple years ago, what is known as the Black Lives Matter Foundation, Inc began. Is was an organization, which had a mission to build local power and to intervene when violence was inflicted on Blck Communities.

Since then, we've commited to imagining and trying to help create a world free of anti-rasicm, where every black person has a power to thrive!

This website is basically about helping you learn more about Black Lives Matter and trying to give you options for how you can help in any way you can to show support for black communities!
